A Love Like Ours

We met in the softest ways, like whispers carried on a breeze.

A spark, a smile, in passing days. As if the universe had keys.

Your hand in mine, a thread that ties. A story written in the skies.

Invisible but ever near, a love that's always been, my dear.

Through tangled roads, through winding lanes. I knew your name, though we hadn't met.

Like melodies that softly play, a song we'd sing without regret.

We danced beneath the stars soft glow. No need for words, just hearts that know."

Not fate, not chance, but something more- A love that waited, at the core.

The world may turn, the seasons change. But here we are, still not estranged.

A love that's timeless, never gone. Like the dawn that greet each morn.
